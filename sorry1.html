English Poem
For You, Swati
Like roses that wilt in the winter's cold air,
My heart aches with guilt beyond what I can bear.
I said words too harsh, I took the wrong way,
And I've thought of nothing but you since that day.
You're the laughter that lights up a room when you walk in,
The warmth that the coldest of mornings begin.
I was foolish, I stumbled, I let my pride speak,
But it's your forgiveness, Swati, that I humbly seek.
If I could go back, I'd choose silence instead,
Or fill every moment with kindness instead.
You deserve all the softness the whole world can give —
To love you and cherish you — that's how I want to live.
So here are my words, laid bare and true,
I'm deeply, sincerely sorry — Swati, it's you.
The one that I treasure, the one that I adore,
Please give me a chance, I won't hurt you anymore. 💕

Swati, tum meri zindagi mein kuch aisa hua ho
jo main words mein bayan nahi kar sakta. 🌸

Jo hua, uske liye mujhe sachchi maafi maangni hai.
Mujhe maaf kar do.

Main jaanta hoon ki maine galti ki,
aur shayad mere shabdon ne tumhe takleef di.
Par believe karo — tumhe hurt karna
kabhi mera irada nahi tha. 💕

Tum mere liye bahut khaas ho Swati.
Tumhari hansi, tumhara pyaar, tumhara saath —
yeh sab mujhe duniya ki sabse badi daulat lagti hai.

Ek baar aur mauka do mujhe,
main wada karta hoon — tum muskurate rehna,
aur main kabhi woh wajah nahi banunga
jo tumhare aankhon mein aansu laaye. 🌹

Tumhara, hamesha. 💗
